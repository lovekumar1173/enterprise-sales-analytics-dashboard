Business Requirement Document (BRD)

Objective:
To analyze enterprise sales performance and identify financial risks
using structured data and analytics.

Business Problems:
- Lack of visibility into revenue trends
- Difficulty identifying high-risk customers
- Delayed payment impact on cash flow

Scope:
- Sales data analysis
- Risk and delay identification
- Business insight generation

Stakeholders:
- Business Analysts
- Finance Team
- Management
